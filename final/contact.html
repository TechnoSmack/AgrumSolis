Contact Information

Location

Brindisi, Puglia, Italy

Located in the heart of the Brindisi Plain, our farm sits within the fertile agricultural landscape of northeastern Puglia, where Mediterranean climate and traditional farming practices create the perfect environment for sustainable citrus cultivation.

Specialization

Sustainable Citrus Farming and Agricultural Tourism

We specialize in environmentally responsible citrus cultivation, combining traditional Mediterranean farming methods with modern sustainability practices to produce exceptional citrus fruits while preserving our agricultural heritage.

Farm Details

30 acres of Mediterranean agricultural land

Our farm encompasses 30 acres of carefully managed agricultural land, featuring traditional citrus groves, integrated olive cultivation, and sustainable farming operations that demonstrate the best of Mediterranean agriculture.

Focus Areas

Citrus fruits, olive cultivation, and sustainable farming practices

We focus on producing high-quality citrus fruits and olive products while implementing regenerative agriculture practices that support soil health, biodiversity, and environmental sustainability.

Regional Context

Heart of Puglia's Agricultural Region

Located in the heart of Puglia's most productive agricultural region, our farm provides visitors with an authentic experience of Mediterranean farming culture. The Brindisi Plain's fertile soils and ideal climate create perfect conditions for understanding traditional and sustainable agriculture.

Brindisi Plain's Fertile Landscape

As part of the Brindisi Plain's fertile agricultural landscape, our farm demonstrates the agricultural practices that have made this region famous for exceptional citrus and olive production. Visitors can experience the natural beauty and productivity of this renowned farming area.

Agricultural Tourism Access

Our location is easily accessible for agricultural tourism and educational visits, making us an ideal destination for those interested in learning about sustainable farming, traditional Mediterranean agriculture, and the cultural heritage of Puglia's farming communities.

Authentic Farm Experience
